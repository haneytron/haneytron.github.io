Today I’d like to talk about LLMs. But first, I’d like to talk about an impressive invention from the 1700s.

The Mechanical Turk

The Mechanical Turk - or “The Turk” as people called it - was an autonomous chess-playing machine. Built in 1770, it went on tour across parts of the world for EIGHTY FOUR (84) YEARS as an automaton that could play a strong and thoughtful game against humans.
